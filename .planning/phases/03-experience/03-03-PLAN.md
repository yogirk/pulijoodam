<objective>
Build an interactive three-lesson tutorial that teaches new players Pulijoodam through guided gameplay on the real engine, with a first-launch prompt and permanent settings access.

Purpose: Pulijoodam is an unfamiliar game to most users. Without a tutorial, new players would be confused by the rules. The tutorial is the primary discoverability mechanism, directly impacting user retention.

Output: Three complete lessons (placement, captures, win conditions), a first-launch modal, a TutorialScreen with overlay guidance, and a "Tutorial" entry in the settings menu.
</objective>
