Rules

3 Tigers vs 15 Goats
Phase 1: Goats place one at a time. Tigers move or capture.
Phase 2: All pieces move. Tigers can still capture.
Capture: Tiger jumps over adjacent goat to empty node (collinear). Chain jumps allowed.
Tigers win: Capture 10 goats.
Goats win: Block all tigers.
